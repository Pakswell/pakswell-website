About Us

Who We Are

Founded in 2015, PAKSWELL has been dedicated to the wooden packaging industry for over 11 years. With self-owned production facilities and warehouses across China, we have established ourselves as a leading manufacturer of EPAL pallets and pallet collars. Over a decade of focused expertise in production and export means we understand exactly what our customers need — from quality packaging solutions to full import & export compliance documentation. We welcome you to visit our factories for inspection and cooperation.

Pallet Collar Hinges

PAKSWELL operates its own dedicated production line for pallet collar hinges. With a monthly production capacity of over 1.5 million pieces, we offer a reliable and consistent supply of high-quality galvanized steel hinges — ensuring full compatibility with our pallet collars and meeting the demands of large-volume buyers worldwide.

Client Confidentiality

We do not display any client names, logos, or partnership details on our website. Protecting the privacy and commercial interests of every partner is a core principle of how we do business. Your trust matters — every partnership detail stays between us.

Export Countries

With over 11 years of export experience, PAKSWELL has an in-depth understanding of country-specific import requirements for European-standard pallets and collars — including approved timber species, ISPM-15 fumigation, and country-of-origin certification (Form A, Form E, FTA, and more). We are well-versed in 20ft and 40ft container loading plans, providing tailored packing calculations and stowage advice based on each customer's order mix and ratio. We regularly export to Korea, Japan, Malaysia, Vietnam, Thailand, the Philippines, and Taiwan, and operate flexibly under FOB, CIF, DAP, and DDP terms.

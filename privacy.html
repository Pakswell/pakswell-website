4. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

4.1 What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device that help us recognize you and improve your experience on our Website.

4.2 Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies: These cookies are necessary for the Website to function properly, including security features and basic navigation.

Analytics Cookies: We use Google Analytics (GA4) to understand how visitors interact with our Website. This information helps us improve our content and user experience. Google Analytics uses cookies to collect anonymous data about your browsing behavior.

4.3 No Advertising Cookies

We do not use cookies for advertising or retargeting purposes. We do not share your data with third-party advertisers.

4.4 Managing Cookies

You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences. Most browsers allow you to refuse cookies or alert you when a cookie is being sent. Please note that disabling essential cookies may affect the functionality of our Website.

5.1 Cloudflare Turnstile

We use Cloudflare Turnstile to prevent spam and abuse on our contact forms. This service may collect information about your interactions to detect suspicious activity. For more information, please review Cloudflare's privacy policy.

5.2 Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics (GA4) to collect and analyze website traffic data. Google Analytics uses cookies and tracking pixels to collect information such as pages visited, time spent, and user demographics. This data is anonymized and used for statistical analysis only.

5.3 Google Fonts

Our Website uses Google Fonts to display typography. When you visit our site, your browser may download font files from Google's servers. Google may collect information about your request in accordance with their privacy practices.

5.4 Web3Forms

We use Web3Forms to process contact form submissions. Information you submit through our contact form is transmitted to Web3Forms' servers for delivery to our email address. Please review Web3Forms' privacy policy for details on how they handle your data.
